How to Connect

  1. Email me at tyler@hallada.net with your Minecraft username to request to join the server
  2. Launch Minecraft (version 1.12.2)
  3. Go to "Multiplayer"
  4. Click "Add Server"
  5. Enter Panic Shack for the "Server Name"
  6. Enter panic-shack.hallada.net for the "Server Address"
  7. Click "Done"
  8. You should now see the server in the list and be able to join it!
